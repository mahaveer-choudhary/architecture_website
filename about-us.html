Her specialization in Vastu Shastra stems from her dedicated learning under various Gurus, complemented by her extensive independent research. This combination enables her to offer personalized Vastu solutions that resonate with the distinct needs of her clients. Her approach integrates traditional wisdom with contemporary architectural and design principles, making her a standout professional in her field.

Her diverse skill set encompasses design software proficiency, academic research, and sustainable development practices, all underpinned by a passion for helping individuals and communities achieve harmonious living environments through Vastu.
